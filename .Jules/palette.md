## 2025-04-08 - Semantic Lists for Screen Reader Navigation
**Learning:** Using `<br>` to separate list-like items (e.g., departure times) prevents screen readers from announcing the total count of items or allowing users to navigate by list item.
**Action:** Always use semantic `<ul>` and `<li>` elements for sets of related items, even if they are simple text strings, and use CSS to manage their presentation.
