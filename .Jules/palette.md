## 2026-04-12 - [Accessible Status Colors]
**Learning:** Standard brand colors like #27ae60 (green) and #3498db (blue) often fail WCAG AA contrast requirements when used with white text. Accessible alternatives like #1b5e20 and #1565c0 provide better readability while maintaining the semantic meaning of the status.
**Action:** Always verify color contrast for status indicators and themed backgrounds, especially when overlaying text.
